### Anabaptist (Mennonite & Amish)

**The Pinecraft community** — the neighborhood along Bahia Vista Street east of downtown — is the largest Amish/Mennonite winter destination in Florida, hosting ~3,000 seasonal residents (Nov–Apr) plus a year-round core. Multiple congregations cluster here.
